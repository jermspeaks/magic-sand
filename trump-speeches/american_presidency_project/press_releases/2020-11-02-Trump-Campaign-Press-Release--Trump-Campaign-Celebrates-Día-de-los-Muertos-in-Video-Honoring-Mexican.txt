The Trump campaign released todaya special videocommemorating Día de los Muertos (Day of the Dead), a major holiday in Mexico and for Mexican-Americans where families gather to pray and honor the lives of their loved ones who have passed. The 30-second video highlights Day of the Dead traditions and cultural icons who are beloved by millions of Mexican Americans and other Latinos.
