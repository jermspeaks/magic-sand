Title: Trump Campaign Press Release - Trump Campaign Launches Sale of Trump 2020 Face Covers
Date: 2020-08-17
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/trump-campaign-press-release-trump-campaign-launches-sale-trump-2020-face-covers
Citation: Donald J. Trump (1st Term), Trump Campaign Press Release - Trump Campaign Launches Sale of Trump 2020 Face Covers Online by Gerhard Peters and John T. Woolley, The American Presidency Project https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/node/345831

President Donald J. Trump's re-election campaign today launched the sale of Trump 2020 face covers in different colors and designs, providing supporters with new ways to show their support for President Trump's re-election while taking safety precautions during the coronavirus outbreak. These face coverings are available on the Donald J. Trump for President, Inc. website. President Trump has been leading the nation through the crisis, taking a whole-of-America approach to fight coronavirus, using the best of public and private resources to slow the spread in the United States. The President urges all patriotic Americans to wear a face cover when they are unable to socially distance.All official merchandise sold in President Trump's online campaign store is proudly made in the United States of America.
