Title: Trump Campaign Statement on Postponed Fayetteville, NC Rally
Date: 2020-10-29
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/trump-campaign-statement-postponed-fayetteville-nc-rally
Citation: Donald J. Trump (1st Term), Trump Campaign Statement on Postponed Fayetteville, NC Rally Online by Gerhard Peters and John T. Woolley, The American Presidency Project https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/node/346829

Because of a wind advisory issued with gusts reaching 50 miles per hour and other weather conditions, the outdoor Fayetteville, NC rally has been postponed until Monday.
