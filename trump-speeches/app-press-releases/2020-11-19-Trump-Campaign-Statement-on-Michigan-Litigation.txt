Title: Trump Campaign Statement on Michigan Litigation
Date: 2020-11-19
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/trump-campaign-statement-michigan-litigation
Citation: Donald J. Trump (1st Term), Trump Campaign Statement on Michigan Litigation Online by Gerhard Peters and John T. Woolley, The American Presidency Project https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/node/346888

"This morning we are withdrawing our lawsuit in Michigan as a direct result of achieving the relief we sought: to stop the election in Wayne County from being prematurely certified before residents can be assured that every legal vote has been counted and every illegal vote has not been counted."- Rudy Giuliani, former Mayor of New York
