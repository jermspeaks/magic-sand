You said if you were president you would work with local police to quell violent riots in our cities, but police organizations have begun to endorse President Trump, sometimes specifically noting that you've sided with rioters against police officers.Now that more than 100 police agencies have pulled out of providing security for the Democratic National Convention in Milwaukee because they'd be deprived of non-lethal tools to control riots, how can you claim that you would work with police and provide them the necessary tools to protect themselves and the public?
