Title: Trump Legal Team Statement on State Certifications
Date: 2020-11-23
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/trump-legal-team-statement-state-certifications
Citation: Donald J. Trump (1st Term), Trump Legal Team Statement on State Certifications Online by Gerhard Peters and John T. Woolley, The American Presidency Project https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/node/346895

"Certification by state officials is simply a procedural step. We are going to continue combatting election fraud around the country as we fight to count all the legal votes. Americans must be assured that the final results are fair and legitimate."- Jenna Ellis, Trump 2020 senior legal advisor
