With the signing of the Great American Outdoors Act, President Trump reaffirmed his commitment to increasing access to the natural beauty of America's public lands. As Joe Biden attacks gun-owners, hunters, and other sportsmen, the Trump Administration is fostering a new generation of outdoorsmen to preserve and protect our country's natural landscape for years to come. Michael Waddell, host of Bone Collector TV, and Willie Robertson, CEO of Duck and Buck Commander, discussed all this and more on tonight's 'Team Trump Online!
