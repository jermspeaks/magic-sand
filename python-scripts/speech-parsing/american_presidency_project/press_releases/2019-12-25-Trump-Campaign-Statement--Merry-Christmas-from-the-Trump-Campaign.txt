Title: Trump Campaign Statement - Merry Christmas from the Trump Campaign
Date: 2019-12-25
URL: https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/documents/trump-campaign-statement-merry-christmas-from-the-trump-campaign
Citation: Donald J. Trump (1st Term), Trump Campaign Statement - Merry Christmas from the Trump Campaign Online by Gerhard Peters and John T. Woolley, The American Presidency Project https://www.presidency.ucsb.edu/node/348498

In celebration of Christmas Day, Lara Trump, Senior Advisor to Donald J. Trump for President, Inc., released the following statement:"Today, as we celebrate the birth of our Savior, I want to offer all Americans the most heartfelt Christmas wishes on behalf of the entire Trump family and the Trump Campaign. We hope the spirit of this season is stronger than ever in your hearts and in your homes. May you enjoy a day filled with peace, love, and joy as we wish you and your loved ones a very Merry Christmas!"
